2. How to register?

Step 1
As a parent you have to register yourself and your child by clicking Register button on home screen.

Step 2
You will be prompted to fill in the details in the form and click Submit button.

That’s it! You are now registered.

3. How to reset Parent’s password?

In the login screen click on “Forgot Password?” link. Enter your email id and submit.

You will get Reset Password Link to your registered e-mail. Please follow the instructions and reset your password. If you don’t see mail in your inbox, please check out your Promotions / Spam folder.

4.What do I do if I forget my child’s login details (user name/ password)?

Login as parent and click on the children menu on left side as shown below.

All the registered children’s username can be found here.
In the children menu, click on Edit.

Please update the password and click update.

5. How to take subscription?

  • After successful registration as parent, you will be prompted to select package of your choice.
  • There are three convenient packages for 11plus subjects and one independent schools previous papers package. Please select the required package and click Buy. Payment is made through secured PayPal payment gateway.

6. How to take tests?

After buying package, login as child. You can see the available tests to your package by clicking Practice tests in the left side of the menu.

In the Practice Tests menu you will find the below screen where you can select the test you want to take.

Select the subject required in the drop down menu of Product and you can take the tests.

Follow the instructions and complete the test. Samples are shown below.

Select the answers for questions and submit the test. Results will be displayed after submitting the test and answers for all the questions will be given.

Important Note: If you login as Parent, it is not possible to take test.

7. How to take FREE DEMO tests?

Please login as child and click on "Practice Tests" left hand side menu and select "Test Type" value as "Free" to see DEMO tests. Click on "Take Test" to start any test.

8. Is there any downloadable material?

11plusehelp provides Children with useful downloadable material. These can be downloaded from the Resources menu.

9. I have taken monthly/quarterly/yearly subscription. Can I take independent school tests?

No. Independent Schools tests package is different from the regular monthly/quarterly/yearly packages. In order to access independent schools previous question papers, you are required to take Independent Schools package separately.

10. Where do I see the tests that I have already attempted?

In the Test History menu, you will find all the tests that child has attempted and scores achieved in each test.
